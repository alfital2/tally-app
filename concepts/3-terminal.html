## FLAGS
What it does.
--ringmenu bar
A dual ring in your menu bar: outer arc = usage, inner = your 5-hour reset window. Recolors green → amber → red as you climb. Always one glance away.
--dashboardon click
Click the ring for the full view: session and weekly usage with live reset countdowns. --bars swaps rings for bars if that's your thing.
--alert=80threshold
Fires a native notification the instant you cross a threshold you set — independently for session and weekly. No surprise cut-offs.
--localalways on
Uses your own Claude login, runs entirely on your Mac, talks only to claude.ai. No servers, no telemetry, no account. Nothing leaves the machine.
## INSTALL
Three commands, basically.
1 · open
Sign in to Claude once, in Tally's built-in window — the same login you already use.
2 · daemonize
Tally checks your session & weekly limits quietly in the background, about once a minute.
3 · glance
Read the ring in your menu bar; click any time for the full dashboard.
