Field report · macOS menu-bar utility

Your Claude limit, finally in plain sight.

Tally is a tiny app that sits in your menu bar and reports your Claude.ai session and weekly usage as two quiet rings — turning from green to red as you approach the wall, so a limit never catches you mid-thought again.

What it reports

// glance

The menu-bar ring

Outer for usage, inner for your 5-hour reset window. Green, yellow, red — read it without clicking, all day.

// expand

The dashboard

Click for session and weekly rings with live reset countdowns. Prefer bars? A single toggle changes everything.

// warn

The threshold alert

Cross a line you set — say 80% — and a notification arrives, separately for session and weekly. No more surprises.

// trust

The privacy

Your own Claude login, kept on your Mac, talking only to claude.ai. No servers, no tracking, nothing in transit.

The price

Free.

Every feature, for everyone, with no catch. The “7-day trial” is a gentle joke — after a week Tally may ask if you'd like to chip in, but it never stops working and nothing is taken away.
