Four quiet jobs.

One-glance ring

Outer arc is usage, inner is your 5-hour reset window. Green → yellow → red as you climb. No clicking required.

Full dashboard

Click for session and weekly rings with live reset countdowns. Prefer bars? One toggle flips the whole view.

Alert before the wall

Set a threshold - say 80% - and get pinged separately for session and weekly. Surprise cut-offs, gone.

Private & local - nothing leaves your Mac

Tally uses your own Claude login, stays on your machine, and talks only to claude.ai. No servers, no accounts, no tracking.
