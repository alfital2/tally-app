// what it does

A few useful things.

Right in your menu bar

A dual ring — outer for usage, inner for your 5-hour reset window — colored green → yellow → red as you climb. Your limit, always one glance away.

A rings dashboard

Click for a glowing view of session and weekly usage, with live reset countdowns. Prefer bars? One toggle switches the whole thing.

Alerts before the wall

Get a notification when you cross a threshold you set — say 80% — separately for session and weekly. No more surprise cut-offs.

Private & local

Tally uses your own Claude login, stays on your Mac, and talks only to claude.ai — no servers, no tracking, nothing leaves your machine.
