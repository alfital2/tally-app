What it does

Everything, nothing more.

Right in your menu bar

A dual ring — outer for usage, inner for your 5-hour reset window — colored green → yellow → red as you climb. Your limit, always one glance away.

A rings dashboard

Click for a glowing Activity-rings view of session and weekly usage, with live reset countdowns. Prefer bars? One toggle switches the whole thing.

Alerts before the wall

Get a notification when you cross a threshold you set — say 80% — separately for the session and the weekly limit. No more surprise cut-offs.

Private & free

Tally uses your own Claude login, stays on your Mac, and talks only to claude.ai — no account, no servers, no tracking, no cost.

How it works

Three steps.

1

Sign in once

Log in to Claude in Tally's built-in window — the same login you already use.

2

It watches

Tally checks your session & weekly limits every minute, quietly in the background.

3

You glance

See the ring in your menu bar; click any time for the full dashboard.
